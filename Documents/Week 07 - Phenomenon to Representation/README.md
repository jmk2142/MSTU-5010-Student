# Week 07 - Mini Assignment: Phenomenon -> Inquiry -> Representation

## Goal
Move from a topic you care about to a clear phenomenon, two inquiry lenses, and one representation idea you can develop for your final project.

## Assignment Prompt
Pick one phenomenon you care about for your final project.
Then complete these three moves.

### 1) Name the phenomenon
Write one sentence:

`When X happens in [context], Y changes.`

Guidelines:
- Use a specific context (classroom, studio, online community, tool workflow, etc.).
- Name an observable change (behavior, artifact quality, decision pattern, participation, interpretation).

### 2) Apply both inquiry lenses (Salomon)
Use both of these sentence stems:

- `Analytically, I can study this by...`
- `Systemically, I can study this by...`

Course readings folder: [Google Drive](https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1kCWXifyNegE5GBZXKlC-oh20U71H6HJi?usp=sharing)

Guidelines:
- Analytic lens: isolate one variable/mechanism.
- Systemic lens: trace relationships and context-level patterns.

### 3) Connect literature to representation
Use at least one source (course reading or credible external source) and identify:
- what phenomenon it represents,
- how it represents it (model, simulation, interface, robot behavior, prompt system, dataset, narrative framing),
- what kind of evidence it uses.

Then propose one representation direction for your own project:
- a **test**, **statement**, or **experience**.

## Deliverable (1 page or 1 slide)
Include the following sections:
1. Phenomenon statement
2. Analytic lens (3-4 lines)
3. Systemic lens (3-4 lines)
4. Source + representation logic (3-5 lines)
5. Your representation idea (2-4 lines)
6. One evidence source you can collect next week

## Success Criteria (minimum)
- The phenomenon is concrete and observable.
- Both analytic and systemic lenses are present and meaningfully different.
- At least one source is used to support representational thinking.
- The proposed representation is clear enough to prototype.
- One realistic evidence path is identified.

## Optional Extension
Bring a short clip/image (fiction, film, comics, games, social media) that stages your phenomenon.
Use it as a comparison case for your representation idea.
